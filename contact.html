Frequently Asked Questions

Quick replies to common queries regarding security and deployment timelines.

What is the typical deployment window for traffic grids?

For standard medium-sized municipal intersections (50-100 signals), deployment takes 8 to 12 weeks. This includes edge camera installation, telemetry testing, and baseline model calibration prior to active routing.

How does Stackly preserve passenger privacy?

We process all data streams at the node level. High-resolution video is immediately deleted and replaced with generic bounding boxes and vector numbers. No facial structures, vehicle plates, or personal identifiers are stored or uploaded.

Can the software integrate with legacy electrical grids?

Yes. Our Green Smart Grid platform utilizes open-standard adapters that interface with legacy SCADA systems and standard power meters, enabling real-time monitoring without grid replacement.
