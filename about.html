Our Growth

Evolution of Stackly

From a green energy research group to a global smart city platform developer.

2022

Foundation & First Research Lab

Stackly was established in Boston as an academic spin-off focused on smart electrical grid routers and localized energy grids.

2024

Initial Municipal Pilot

Deployed our first dynamic traffic sensor matrix across 12 main junctions in Newark, cutting fuel idling metrics by 18%.

2026

Global Expansion

Secured municipal contracts in 14 international metro zones. Launch of our unified Smart City Edge Operating System.

Our Mission

To deliver resilient, privacy-first, and zero-carbon digital infrastructure platforms that enable metropolitan administrations to maximize community wellbeing while meeting aggressive carbon-neutral targets.

Our Vision

To establish the global software standard for urban operating systems, turning every streetlight, substation, and water artery into an automated, self-healing, and sustainable asset.
